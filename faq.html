What's Alice's story?

Alice follows a white rabbit into its hole and comes to a magical, imaginative and exciting world. She experiences some gorgeous and thrilling adventures on her journey through Wonderland. She meets new and marvelous creatures like the White Rabbit, the Mad Hatter, and the Cheshire Cat on her way through this fascinating world...

Who are the main figures?

Alice is the main character of the stories as she heads through Wonderland.

The White Rabbit with its clothes and pocket clock is the reason for Alice's journey.

Cheshire Cat, famous for its grin, is one of Alice companions.

The Mad Hatter meets Alice at the famous tea party and from there on is part of her adventures.

Of course there are many more characters within the story! To get more information about them take a look into the fabulous books or just look them up on the internet!

What's Pocket Code?

Pocket Code allows you to create your own games, animations, interactive music videos, and many kind of other apps, directly on your phone or tablet. You can download Pocket Code for free on Google Play. We've got a great community site where you can find all programs which were created with Pocket Code. Pay us a visit and have a look around.

How to get into Pocket Code?

We have some great beginners information you should check out. Or try out our hour of code tutorial.

Is it free?

Your participation in the Alice Game Jam is totally free.

Our Pocket Code app is perpetually provided free of any charge on Google Play and will never include any in-App purchases or ads. You can download all programs made with Pocket Code by others from all over the world without any registration. You set up an account with us only when you upload your own first program you created with Pocket Code.

Who is behind Pocket Code?

Pocket Code is provided by the non-profit Catrobat organization which aim is to provide free educational apps. Around 300 volunteers from all over the world work on the project to create apps and possibilities for teenagers and education. More information can be found on our website www.catrobat.org.

Which content, sounds or graphics I may use?

We don't restrict you in any way on such creative decisions. Nevertheless please be aware of the right of others, so please make sure you are allowed to use any content, and give attribution to the work of others you have used in the description of your submission. There are many free sources which offer content under Creative Commons or similar open-to-use licenses. A good source to find content under a Creative Common license is search.creativecommons.org.

Where can I find information about licenses in the Game Jam?

All programs and content submitted by yourself as part of the Alice Game Jam, unless specified otherwise, is licensed under free open source and creative commons licenses. These licenses allow the redistribution and adaptation of the material for all purposes as long as it is properly attributed and shared under the same license.

You can find detailed information about licenses on developer.catrobat.org.

What do I have to bear in mind if I want to participate?

The Theme, Rules, Information about the Jury and many more Details which are important about the Jam can be found on our website under "Event" and "Participate". To be part of our Alice Game Jam just follow these instructions and that's it!

Where do I have to register and submit my app?

To register for the Jam special links to a form are provided at "Participate". All information will be handled with care - more information at Terms&Privacy.

To submit your finished game please tag it with #AliceGameJam in the description of the project and upload it from Pocket Code. You can upload revised versions of your game under the same name and account as often as you want.

How will it go on after we submitted our game?

After the deadline we will forward the submitted games to our expert jury who will take a closer look on it. They will take several things into consideration to find the best games. After that we will inform you via E-mail if you're the winner. But to keep you up to date we will provide the current status on our social media feeds and newsletter!

Who will judge the game entries and what are the prizes?

The jury will be announced at our website right before the Jam!

Of course there will be rewards and prizes for the best games. More information on this will be announced once it is decided!

How can I contact you?

If you need any further information, advice or anything else, you may contact us via our social media sites which are linked on the bottom. You may also use the "Contact" link on the bottom to write us an E-mail which we will try to answer as soon as possible.

Is there any possibility to get news and information about this Game Jam?

To keep you up to date we provide regular updates on our social media pages. Additionally you can subscribe our newsletter to get all news and updates for the Alice Game Jam!


We wish you lots of fun and good luck for this jam!
