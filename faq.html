FAQs

What's Alice Story?

The young girl Alice follows a white rabbit into its hole and comes to a magical, imaginative and exciting world. She experiences some gorgeous and thrilling adventures on her journey through the wonderland. She meets new and marvelous creatures like the White Rabbit, the Mad Hatter and Cheshire cat on her way through the doors of this fascinating world she faced at the beginning of her story...

Who are the main figures?

Alice is the main character of this stories and heads through the wonderland.

The White Rabbit with its clothes and pocket clock is the reason for Alices journey.

Cheshire Cat, famous for its grin, is one of Alice companions at these stories.

The mad Hatter meets Alice at the famous tea party and gets from there on part of her stories.

Of course there are many more characters within the stories! To get more information about them take a look into the fabulous books or just look them up on the internet!

What's Pocket Code?

Pocket Code allows you to create your own games, animations, interactive music videos, and many kind of other apps, directly on your phone or tablet. You can download Pocket Code for free on Google Play. We've got a great community site where you can find all programs, which were created with Pocket Code. Pay us a visit and have a look around.

How to get into Pocket Code?

We have some great beginners programs you should check out. Or try out our tutorial.

Is it for free?

Pocket Code is provided for free on Google Play and comes without any in-App purchase or advertises. You can login directly with your Google-Account to upload your projects, so you evend don't need any further registration.

Who is behind Pocket Code?

Pocket Code is provided by the non-profit Catrobat project, which aim it is so provide free educational apps. More over 100 volunteers all over the world work on the project to create even more apps and possibilities for teenagers and education. More information may be found on our Catrobat website www.catrobat.org.

What do I have to bear in mind if I want to participate?

The Theme, Rules, Information about the Jury and many more Details which are important about the Jam can be found on our website at "Event" and "Participate". To be part of our AliceGameJam just follow these instructions and that's it!

Where do I have to register and submit my app?

To register for the Jam special links to a form are provided at "Participate". Please sign up all information asked, which of course will be handled with care - more information at Terms&Privacy.

To submit your finished game please tag it with #AliceGameJam and upload it within Pocket Code.

How it will go on after we submitted our game?

After the deadline we will forward the submitted games to our expert jury who will take a closer look on it. They will take several things into consideration to find the best games. After that we will inform you via E-mail if you're the winner. But to keep you up to date we will provide the current status on our social media feeds and newsletter!

Who will judge the projects and what are the prizes?

We will ask a couple of experts in its fields to take a look at your games and judge them. The jury will be published at our website right before the Jam!

Of course there will be some rewards and prizes for the best games. More information on that will be available soon enough before on our website!

How can I contact you?

If you need any further information, advise or anything else you may contact us via our social media sites which are linked on the bottom. However, you may also use the "Contact" link on the bottom to write us an E-mail which we try to answer as soon as possible.

Is there any possibility to get news and information about #AliceGameJam?

To keep you up to date we provide regular updates on our social media pages. Additionally you can sign up our newsletter to get all relative news and updates of our Pocket Game Jam!
