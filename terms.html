Privacy Policy

USING CATROBAT SOFTWARE

Your privacy and safety online are very important to us. To protect your privacy, we limit what we collect and what we publish on our websites.

You do not have to tell us anything about yourself to download Catrobat application software. When you download Catrobat software or sign up to a Catrobat website, we give you the option of telling us some things about yourself, but you do not have to fill in any of this information if you want to.

USING OUR WEBSITES

You can try out user contributed programs on our Pocket Code website without registering or providing any information. As you use our websites, we collect some data on where you click and which parts of the sites you visit. This 'click data' helps us figure out ways to improve the websites. We do not share your information with anyone.

Terms of use and service

While participating in this Alice Game Jam, you are may share programs and ideas with people:

  • from many different countries and cultures
  • of all ages (from young children to teens and grandparents)
  • with all levels of experience

We need your help to make this Game Jam and its associated community a supportive place for every member. Here's how you can help:

  • Be respectful. When participating, please remember that people of many different ages and backgrounds will see your contributions.
  • Give credit. Feel free to make modified versions of other people's programs - just make sure to give them credit. One place to give credit is in your program's description. Please see the paragraph below this dotted list for details about modifying programs of other users.
  • Be honest. Don't pretend to be someone other than who you are or try to trick the community.
  • Keep personal information private. Don't share your email address, phone number, or other personal contact information to anyone beyond the organizers of the Game Jam.
  • Help keep the Game Jam friendly. If you feel others would find a program or comment mean, insulting, too violent, or otherwise inappropriate, please report it to alice@alicegamejam.com together with a short explanation and a link to the place where you found it. We will review your report and may remove any program, comment, or post.

Through the Alice Game Jam we want to foster cooperation and allow everyone's creativity to combine for the benefit of all of humanity.   • By uploading a program to our websites, you accept and agree to put this work under certain free licenses of uploaded Catrobat programs. Your work will thus benefit the whole world and remain free for everyone to use, a bit similar to text and pictures freely contributed to Wikipedia by volunteers from all over the world. Think of it as your present to humanity: you give to everyone, and all others who upload their work to our websites give to you.
